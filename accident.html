Mr. Reisman has been active for over two decades in representing injured bicyclists and defending bicyclists wrongly charged in Traffic Citations. He serves as legal counsel to Dirt Rag and Bicycle Times Magazines. He is a member of the League of American Bicyclists Legal Affairs Committee, Bike Pittsburgh and the WPW Bicycle Club.


ACCIDENTS CAN HAPPEN. HERE ARE 5 THINGS THAT YOU SHOULD DO:

1. Get medical attention immediately.

2. Record license numbers, makes, models, years and owners of all vehicles.

3. Get names, addresses, phone numbers and insurance information from drivers, passengers and witnesses.

4. Inform the police and wait for their arrival.

5. To protect your legal rights, consult your attorney.
