Practices / Liquor Licensing Lawyer

Any matter involving liquor Licensing, we have you covered.

Mr. Reisman has served as Special Liquor Counsel to the Urban Redevelopment Authority of Allegheny County, the Regional Development Funding Corporation and the Steel Valley Enterprise Zone. His column on liquor licensing matters, View from the Bar, appears regularly in The Independent Restaurateur. He has also been published in the Pittsburgh Legal Journal and the Allegheny Lawyer.

We've helped restaurateurs, entertainment venues, hotels, bars, beer distributorships, and many other entities in Liquor Licensing.

Our capabilities Include the purchase and sale of liquor licenses and licensed businesses, compliance and contractual matters, real estate transactions and entity formation. Also transfers of liquor licenses; applications for new licenses; license renewals and defense of PLCB objections to license renewals; Citation defense; appeals; advice and guidance regarding compliance with the Liquor Code and PLCB Regulations and policies.
