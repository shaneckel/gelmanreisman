Practices / Wills And Estates Lawyer

Gelman & Reisman provides counsel and representation in a full range of Elder Law matters.

Personal attention is the foundation of the firm and we mean that. Our clients are not handed to associates. Attorney Bruce Gelman will always provide the personal attention each case and client requires. We will also continue to keep fees for simple documents at a reasonable amount.

    Non-complex testamentary documents include:

  • – Simple Last Will and Testament
  • – Living Wills
  • – Will with Non-Complex Testamentary Trust

    Provide asset protection and management:

  • – Establishing Trusts in Wills
  • – Revocable and Irrevocable Trusts
  • – Special Needs Trusts
  • – Life Insurance Trusts
  • – Estate and Trust Administration
  • – Minimizing Estate
  • – Gift and Transfer taxes

Every day we make healthcare decisions without any problem. However, there will be a time when you are unable to make essential healthcare decisions due to physical or mental limitations or incompetency.

    Pre-planning is essential. Gelman & Reisman can provide the following services: Comprehensive Durable Powers of Attorney; Healthcare Directives; Living Will; Guardianship Special Needs Trusts.

Gelman & Reisman also represents Executors and Administrators in all estate probate administration.

We take these matters seriously. For more information regarding Wills and Estates law, please contact us.
