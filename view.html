LIQUOR LICENSING

Mr. Reisman represents restaurateurs, entertainment venues, hotels, bars, beer distributorships and other entities in Liquor Licensing and related matters, including the purchase and sale of liquor licenses and licensed businesses, compliance and contractual matters, real estate transactions and entity formation. Mr. Reisman has served as Special Liquor Counsel to the Urban Redevelopment Authority of Allegheny County, the Regional Development Funding Corporation and the Steel Valley Enterprise Zone. His column on liquor licensing matters, View from the Bar, appears regularly in The Independent Restaurateur. He has also been published in the Pittsburgh Legal Journal and the Allegheny Lawyer.
