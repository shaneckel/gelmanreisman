Will and Estates Lawyer

Gelman & Reisman provides counsel and representation in a full range of Elder Law matters.

One on one attention is the foundation of the firm. Our clients are not handed to associates. Attorney Gelman will always provide the personal attention each case and client requires. G&R will also continue to keep fees for simple documents at a reasonable amount.

Non-complex testamentary documents include:

Simple Last Will and Testament, Living Wills, Will with non-complex testamentary trust.

Gelman & Reisman provides asset protection and management through a variety of methods:

Establishing trusts in Wills, Revocable and Irrevocable Trusts, Special Needs Trusts, Life Insurance Trusts, Estate and Trust Administration, Minimizing estate, gift and transfer taxes.

Every day we make healthcare decisions without any problem. However, there will be a time when you are unable to make essential healthcare decisions due to physical or mental limitations or incompetency.

Pre-planning is essential with the following services:

Comprehensive Durable Powers of Attorney, Healthcare Directives, Living Will, Guardianship, Special Needs Trusts.

Gelman & Reisman also represents Executors and Administrators in all estate probate administration.
